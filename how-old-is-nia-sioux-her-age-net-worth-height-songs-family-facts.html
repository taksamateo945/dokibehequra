Nia Sioux Bio & Family: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She is known for her appearance on the reality-TV dancing series Dance Moms (2011-2017) alongside her daughter.

Nia’s Career As A Dancer & Singer: Songs, Net Worth

For her versatile career, the beautiful and talented youngster bags a compelling net worth that dwells above $1 million. The major part of her earnings comes from the dancing reality-TV show, Dance Moms, where she featured for six seasons from 2011 to 2017.

Nia was just 3 when she began dancing. Subsequently, at the age of 10, she joined the show Dance Moms. While she was pursuing her dancing career, she found a passion for music as well. As a result, she released her first single titled Star In Your Own Life in 2015. Since then, she has released a number of songs including Slay, Here for You, Dance, Low Key Love, and so on.

Apart from that, Nia is also known for honing her acting skills. She has made an appearance in a number of TV series including The Bold and the Beautiful (2018-2019) and Sunnyside Up (2019-2020). She is now set to appear in two movies named The Lies I Tell Myself and I Am Mortal.
