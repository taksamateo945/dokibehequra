McDonald's All-Day Breakfast Has Arrived and the Internet Is Lovin' It

April 2024 · 2 minute read

There’s evidence of water on Mars and McDonald’s is now serving breakfast all day — it’s all happening, people. The fast-food chain kicked off its Breakfast All-Day menu on Tuesday, Oct. 6, and the Internet is Mmm…lovin’ it.

 

This means you can now have hash browns, eggs, sausage, pancakes, and all the Egg McMuffins your heart desires at any time of the day. Well, actually, there’s a catch, which the Internet has also been quick to point out, because you can’t get all the items on the menu all day, only some. Meaning the beloved Mickey D’s fan-favorite Bacon, Egg, and Cheese McGriddle isn’t available when hunger strikes at 3:45 p.m.

Also, depending on where you live, you’re either Team McMuffin (California and pretty much the rest of America) or Team Biscuit (Georgia and, like, three other chosen states), because yes, this menu is limited.

If you’re trying to watch your waistline, you’re also out of luck because the Egg White Delight McMuffin is also not offered past normal brekky hours.

For those of you who live for hash browns, spokeswoman Lisa McComb (yes, her last name is McComb) reassured CNBC that they should be available at a “vast majority,” or about 90 percent, of locations all day. (Sorry, 10 percent.)
