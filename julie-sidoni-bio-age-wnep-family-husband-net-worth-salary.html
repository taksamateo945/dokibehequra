Julie Sidoni Biography

Julie Sidoni is an American journalist presently working as an anchor and reporter for Newswatch 16 at 12, 4, and 5 on WNEP-TV. She also handles and manages all the station’s Healthwatch 16 reasons. Furthermore, she is working as a reporter for the National Public Radio affiliate WDUQ-FM based in Pittsburgh.

Sidoni attended Central Catholic High School. After completing her high school education, she attended and graduated from Duquesne University in Pittsburgh.

Julie joined the WNEP-TV station team in February 2002. Previously, she served as a reporter at WJAC-TV in Johnstown, where she reported for a period of one and a half years after graduating. At the station, she covered the September 11 terror attacks on New York City, Washington, D.C., and Western Pennsylvania. Moreover, she also served as a reporter for WTAE-TV in Pittsburgh and for WDUQ-FM, the National Public Radio affiliate in Pittsburgh.
