Heyy Maid: Joseline Hernandez Fans Believe Shes Throwing Shade at Mimi Faust in This Post

June 2024 · 3 minute read

It seems Joseline Hernandez could be stirring the pot with her former arch-nemesis Mimi Faust.

The reality TV star posted one of her infamous lines from “Love and Hip Hop: Atlanta” as a caption this week. She took to Instagram on Wednesday with a photo of herself barefaced and in a robe. Hernandez captioned her post, “Hey maid.”

Many fans instantly thought Hernandez was shading Faust.

“Where Mimi. 🤔 Molly the maid 🤣”

“I see you got ur maid outfit on like you always do, I don’t blame you you gotta clean 😂”

“Heyyyyy maidddd the caption 💀💀 all time fav scene EVER ! 😂 i know mimi somewhere mad🤣🤣”

“💀 I can’t with you @joseline we on lockdown you got me ready to swing from the shower rod dead as f–k😂”

“I see got on ya Maid outfit like you always do 🤣😍 iconic Pls dont come for mimi or her maid outfit😭😂”

“Hey maid,” became a signature line of Hernandez’s during season 2 of “LHHATL” when she caught Faust staying at Stevie J.’s home, a music producer they were both in a relationship with at the same time without knowing so. Hernandez greeted Faust by saying, “Hey, maid, I see you got on your maid outfit like you always do.”

Although Faust didn’t comment on Hernandez’s current post, she did admit to VH1 in a recent interview that the “hey, maid” scene with Hernandez still bothered her, considering that she didn’t know Stevie J. was cheating on her with Hernandez.

“That was the worst part about it, is that it was on TV,” Faust said. “If you go through something with your significant other and he’s cheating on you, and it’s just you three going through it and maybe your girlfriends knowing about it — but to go through that and the whole f–kin’ world knows about it and everybody has an opinion? I was at that point ‘the stupidest,’ ‘you’re a doormat,’ you’re this, you’re that. From an audience perspective, I guess that’s how they’re looking at it. They thought because this is TV I know what’s going on. I had no f–kin’ clue. And then everybody wants to ask me why the f–k am I so angry. Are you f–kin’ kidding me? Would you not be angry if this was happening to you.”

Both Hernandez and Faust have moved on from that past love triangle. Hernandez shares 3-year-old Bonnie Bella with Stevie and Faust has 10-year-old daughter Eva Giselle with him also. The music producer co-parents well with both women. Hernandez and Faust, however, seemed to still have a shaky relationship with each other.
