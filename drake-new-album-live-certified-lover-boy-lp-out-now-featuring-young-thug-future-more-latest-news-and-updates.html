Drake new album LIVE Certified Lover Boy LP OUT NOW featuring Young Thug, Future &amp; more late

May 2024 · 8 minute read

DRAKE shared the track list for his new album Certified Lover Boy hours before CLB album FINALLY drops.

Just two hours before the highly anticipated album’s release, Drake retweeted a Twitter post that revealed the track list that includes 21 songs.

The album begins with a track called Champagne Poetry, while other songs include Papi’s Home, Girls Want Girls, You Only Live Twice, The Remorse, and more.

Ahead of the album’s release, billboards across the world revealed who would feature on Certified Lover Boy.

Each billboard was shown in the place each artist is from.

Features include Yebba, Tema, Jay-Z, 21 Savage, Yung Thug, and more.

Meanwhile, the rapper, 34, left listeners baffled with the bizarre album artwork for Certified Lover Boy – designed by British artist Damien Hirst – featuring 12 emojis of pregnant women.

Drake had initially intended to drop Certified Lover Boy in January this year – but is instead released it on Friday.

Drake’s bombshell announcement came on the heels of Kanye West finally delivering his 10th studio album, Donda.
