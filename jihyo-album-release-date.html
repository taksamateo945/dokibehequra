Who Is Jihyo?

Meet Jihyo, also known by her real name Park Ji-hyo, a remarkably gifted South Korean singer and the esteemed leader of the renowned K-pop girl group, TWICE.

Back in 2015, JYP Entertainment brought this talented group together, and since then, they have soared to incredible heights of fame.

Jihyo, born on February 1, 1997, in Guri, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea, has emerged as a prominent figure in the dynamic world of K-pop.

In her role as TWICE’s leader, Jihyo has showcased exceptional leadership, guiding the group to unparalleled success.

However, her talents extend far beyond leadership; she also takes center stage as the group’s main vocalist.

Her commanding and unique voice has earned her adoration from both fans and critics, and her magnetic presence on stage captivates audiences all over the globe.

Now, after years of honing her skills and winning hearts as part of TWICE, Jihyo is ready to step into the limelight on her own.

The excitement is tangible as she prepares to make her solo debut with her first mini-album “ZONE,” scheduled for release on August 18, 2023.

With her exceptional talent, undeniable charisma, and unyielding passion for music, Jihyo’s solo venture is undeniably one of the most highly anticipated and thrilling milestones in her flourishing career.
