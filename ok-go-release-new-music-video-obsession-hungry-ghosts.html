OK Go Release New Music Video For &quot;Obsession&quot; from 'Hungry Ghosts'

July 2024 · 2 minute read

OK Go has released a music video for their song, “Obsession” from their Hungry Ghosts album.  The album was released back in October of 2014, but the band has created some interesting music videos for some of its songs since its release.

The last two music videos released by OK Go were for “Upside Down & Inside Out” and “The One Moment.”  Throughout their career, the band has made several one of a kind music videos and this is no exception. The band used 567 printers and tons of paper to create interesting visual effects.  However, none of that paper has gone to waste because the band states at the beginning of the video that all the paper has already been recycled.

We think the visual effects created using the printed paper look absolutely amazing!  The band encourages you to watch the video at 1440p or 2160p.  Those resolutions really make the colors stand out.

Note: The video has a lot of flashing colors so be careful if you are susceptible to seizures.
